Questions, answered

Is Prism free?

Yes — free while it's in beta. No card, and no account needed just to use it.

Does my audio go to the cloud?

No. Recording, transcription, and the write-up all happen on your own device. Your audio never leaves your computer.

Do I need to install anything else?

No. On Mac it's a single app in your menu bar; on Windows everything it needs comes bundled with the installer.

Does it work offline?

Yes — recording, transcribing, and note-writing all work with no internet. If you sync, it catches up next time you're online.

What can it record?

Anything you can hear on your machine — Zoom, Meet, Teams, a phone call, or a conversation in the room. Only when you press record.

Does it join meetings like a bot?

Never. Prism notices a call and offers to record from your own device with one click. Nothing ever joins your meeting.

Can I use Claude instead of the local AI?

Yes. Connect your own Claude to write the notes, or keep it fully local. You can also let Claude read your Prism notes back.

How does syncing work?

Sign in with your email and your notes, folders, and photos stay in sync across Mac, Windows, and iPhone. Signed out, everything stays only on your device.

Prism is a free app from outside the App Store, so macOS asks you to approve it once. It takes about ten seconds. On Windows, SmartScreen shows a similar warning — click More info → Run anyway.
