Opening Prism the first time

Prism is a free app from outside the App Store, so macOS asks you to approve it once. It takes about ten seconds. On Windows, SmartScreen shows a similar warning — click More info → Run anyway.

📁
Step 1
Drag Prism to Applications

Open the downloaded Prism.dmg and drag the Prism icon into your Applications folder.

Step 2
Click Done — don't trash it

Step 3
Open Anyway, once

Go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click Open Anyway. Prism opens normally from then on.
